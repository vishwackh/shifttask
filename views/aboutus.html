About

 

What happens when 4 IIT graduates meet? An amazing company is conceptualized!

Meet our founders - Aseem Khare, Abhiroop Medhekar, Ajay Bhatt and Amit Chahalia.

These 4 guys realized that people are spending their precious free time on managing household chores instead of pursuing their passions! The problem also existed on service providers' side who had to struggle with minimal training and poor earning potential. Upset but elated, that they had a problem to solve, they set out to create a one stop solution for all household needs. And thus, Taskbob was founded in December 2014 with the vision of creating happy households!

Today, Taskbob provides a range of home services like home cleaning, car spa, handymen services, drivers, appliance repair, maids on demand & the recently launched, at-home beauty services. To ensure that customers have a delightful experience, the service experts are chosen after a thorough background verification & once hired, undergo extensive training. Taskbob started out serving the households Powai but now gladly serves households all over Mumbai. Taskbob as a brand, has now become synonymous with reliability & quality in Mumbai. The journey so far has been exciting & invigorating, but we are only just getting started!
